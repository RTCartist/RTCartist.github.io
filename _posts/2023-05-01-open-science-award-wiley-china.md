I'm pleased to have received the Open Science High Contribution Author Award from Wiley China. This award recognizes my contributions to open science practices and the dissemination of research findings in the field of neuromorphic computing and emerging memory technologies.

This recognition emphasizes the importance of making research accessible and contributing to the broader scientific community through open publication practices.
