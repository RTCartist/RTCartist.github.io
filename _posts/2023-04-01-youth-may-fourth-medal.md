I'm deeply honored to have received the Youth May Fourth Medal from Beihang University, ranking first among over 10,000 students. This prestigious award recognizes outstanding academic achievement, research excellence, and contributions to the university community.

The Youth May Fourth Medal is one of the highest honors for students at Beihang University, celebrating the spirit of innovation, dedication, and excellence that defines the May Fourth Movement tradition.
